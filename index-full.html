Why do WeCount?

As a society, we are only as safe as our most vulnerable members.
The leading cause of poverty and suffering is a lack of personal community support, the failing of the social safety-net that keeps us all from falling between the cracks.

WeCount is designed to strengthen our social safety-net by helping people connect and support each other with resources and donations within our communities.

What Role Does WeCount Play?

WeCount connects you or someone you know with available local resources

SEEK PERSONAL SERVICES

We help individuals and families learn about services, events and resources in their neighborhood that they may not be aware of. WeCount provides a quick an easy way to discover the agencies and community in your area that can contribute to your success!

LEND A HAND

Use your smart phone to help an individual without access to a phone or computer. After someone completes our questionnaire process, they can have the information sent directly via SMS, to an email account, or sign up for their own free WeCount account to track their needs, donations, and appointments!

CONNECT TO LOCAL RESOURCES

WeCount works closely with the local, regional and federally-directed agencies, faith-based and community organizations. WeCount provides consistently accurate listing of local resources, their contact information, as well as their eligibility and availability.
