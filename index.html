Want to find out why WeCount?

Yes, I count! Sign Me Up

WeCount isn’t just about getting someone the sleeping bag they need, but also the coffee maker that helps them wake up, keep their job, and stay housed.

As a society, we are only as safe as our most vulnerable members. A leading cause of poverty and suffering is a lack of personal community support, the failing of the social safety net that keeps us all from falling between the cracks.

How It Works

All that’s required to start using WeCount is a confirmed form of contact—either an email or text-enabled phone number

After creating an account, users can choose to seek help or donate

Seek Help

Those seeking help may request up to 5 items from a pre-approved list that includes several categories:

  • Outdoor Gear

  • Home Goods

  • Personal Care Items

  • Children’s Needs

  • Essential Clothing

Donate

Those looking to donate gently-used items can:

  • Offer what they have on hand

  • View specific requests from their neighbors

Once donations have been selected, we provide information about a safe and secure public drop-off site

After the exchange has been made, we inform donors about additional volunteer or donation opportunities

Members are encouraged to both donate and ask for their needs as necessary

WeCount helps communities succeed together.
